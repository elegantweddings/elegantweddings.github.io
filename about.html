F.A.Qs
Wedding Questions

From timelines to traditions, couples have thoughtful questions. We’ve planned and produced Indian weddings since 2007, blending culture with contemporary design and meticulous execution. Below are answers to the most common questions we receive.

Expect transparent quotes, a single point of contact, and a calm, organized team managing vendors, hospitality and production—so your family can stay present and enjoy every moment.

  • Why Elegant Weddings? What sets you apart?
    Since 2007 we’ve designed culturally rooted, highly organized celebrations with a single point of contact. Our production depth, transparent pricing and guest experience focus mean you enjoy the day while we run the show behind the scenes.
  • How do we start? What does the planning journey look like?
    We begin with discovery calls, then share a detailed scope and mood boards. Once aligned, we finalize timelines, vendor lineup and layouts. You get regular updates and walkthroughs all the way to show day rehearsals and execution.
  • Can you work with our family’s traditions and rituals?
    Absolutely. We collaborate with your families and priests to respect rituals, sequencing and etiquette—while elevating the experience with thoughtful design and hospitality.
  • Will we have to coordinate vendors ourselves?
    No. We handle vendor sourcing, negotiations, briefing and on‑day coordination. You approve the selections and we operate end‑to‑end to deliver on time and to quality.
  • What is the best way to get a quote?
    Share your dates, city, estimated guest count and priorities via our contact form. We’ll respond within 24 hours with next steps and a tailored proposal.
