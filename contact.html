F.A.Qs
Wedding Questions

Planning a wedding raises real questions about costs, timelines and guest experience. We’re here to provide clarity, not confusion—grounded in 17+ years of execution.

Browse the FAQs below or reach out—our team will respond within 24 hours and guide you through the next steps.

  • Do you offer planning, decor or full-service?
    We offer end‑to‑end wedding planning, decor and production. You can hire us for complete planning or for decor/production only. Either way, we handle timelines, vendors and on‑ground execution so you can enjoy with your family.
  • How early should we book? Do you do destination weddings?
    Most couples book 6–12 months in advance. We plan weddings across India and overseas. Our team manages venue permits, local vendors, guest logistics and customs compliance for seamless destination experiences.
  • What budgets do you work with? Are there hidden costs?
    We tailor proposals to your priorities and budget. You receive transparent quotes with detailed inclusions and exact deliverables—no hidden costs. Vendor payments can be routed directly or via us as you prefer.
  • Who will be our point of contact? What happens on the day?
    You’ll have a dedicated producer from day one. On the wedding days, our on‑ground team runs the timeline, coordinates all vendors, manages artists, hospitality and guest movement— while your family stays present in the moments.
  • How do you make the decor reflect our culture and story?
    We begin with discovery sessions to understand your story, traditions and aesthetics. Then we create mood boards and a cohesive design plan—covering florals, fabrics, lighting and production details—followed by samples/mock‑ups before execution.
  • Do you handle guest logistics and hospitality?
    Yes. We arrange hotel blocks, airport/venue transport, welcome desks, check‑in support and guest communications. Our hospitality team ensures every guest feels guided and cared for.
