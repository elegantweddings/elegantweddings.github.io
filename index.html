Who we are
Elegant Weddings

Elegant Weddings is led by a couple who are obsessed with details and driven with passion to create an impact in Indian weddings. Our love for people, weddings and culture has helped curate beautiful, customised weddings across the country since 2007.

From intimate ceremonies to big‑fat‑Indian weddings, our team is as excited as you are to design and implement a day that reflects your story and vision.

We handle end‑to‑end planning, decor, and production so you can enjoy your wedding with your loved ones while we manage every detail seamlessly.
