F.A.Qs
Wedding Questions

You want clarity on scope, budgets and deliverables. Our team shares detailed proposals, mood boards and timelines, then manages vendors and on‑ground execution to the minute.

Whether it’s full planning or decor/production only, we build to approved designs with transparent pricing and robust contingency plans.

  • Can we customize a package for our events?
    Absolutely. We build a bespoke scope for each celebration—haldi, sangeet, mehendi, wedding, reception—so you pay for what you value most. We’ll propose options with clear inclusions for planning, decor and production.
  • What is included in decor & production?
    Concept design, stage/mantap, entrances, seating lounges, florals, fabrics, lighting, trussing, sound, special effects and on‑site crew to build, operate and strike—delivered to approved mood boards and layouts.
  • How do you manage vendors and quality?
    We work with vetted partners. Every vendor gets a brief, timeline and responsibility matrix. Our producers run rehearsals, HSE checks and quality gates at build, pre‑show and handover.
  • Do you assist with artist bookings and MC/DJ?
    Yes. We curate entertainment to fit your vibe—from live bands and dhol to DJs and MCs— and manage stage cues, sound checks and rider requirements end‑to‑end.
  • What happens if it rains or plans change?
    We build contingency plans for weather and schedule shifts, including covered setups, alternate layouts, buffer crew and backup power. A risk register is tracked live during show days to protect your experience.
